Site Name: Modern Visions Photography

This name was selected to convey innovation and creativity, which align with the agency's branding. It reflects a forward-thinking approach to photography and videography projects.

Site Purpose

The website will showcase a portfolio of photography and videography projects, provide a platform for clients to book services, and share resources for aspiring photographers and videographers.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A potential client wants to browse the portfolio and book a photo or video shoot online.
  • Scenario 2: An aspiring photographer is looking for tips and resources to improve their skills.

Color Schema

The following colors will be used throughout the site:

  • Dark Gray (#2B2B2B): Used for text and headings.
  • Golden Yellow (#FFD700): Used for accents and call-to-action buttons.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for the main background.

Typography

The selected fonts for the site are:

  • Heading Font: Lora - Used for headings to give a professional and elegant feel.
  • Body Font: Open Sans - Used for paragraphs and other body text for readability.
